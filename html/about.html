Life Story

     Grew up in the beautiful city of Portland Oregon where I attended public schools in the city of Gladstone OR for grades k-8. From a very early age, I was very interested in chemistry and engineering while I cursed biology. I participated in many sports such a soccer, baseball, and football and was also a part of the school’s band playing trombone, tuba, and drums. However, my education was much more important to me and I became a straight A student in my later years in middle school. 
     
     I then was accepted into La Salle Catholic College Preparatory in Milwaukie OR where I received my high school diploma and graduated with academic awards in math and theater along with a few college credits. I was deeply engaged with the theater department taking on many roles such as stage manager, lighting crew member, actor, and more. I began to remove myself from this community as I began working at a retail store. I was able to manage school, friends, work, and homework surprising well. As for my last summer, I took on a second full time job working for the Oregon City Police Department doing many different jobs such as ticketing, metering, enforcing code, painting, filing, and much more. I packed up my car with my belongings, said goodbye, and left for Tacoma Washington.
    
     I narrowly chose to attend Tacoma Community College due to financial reasons and would later relize it would be one of my biggest regrets. However, I pushed forward and spent two years and earned the credits for an associates in computer science. I then quickly transferred over to the University of Washington and was accepted in the institute of technology.  
    
    So with that, I am now currently a student at the University of Washington living in Tacoma WA, earning a Bachelor’s of Science in Computer Software Systems. Scheduled to graduate spring of 2017 and currently looking for internships and or jobs. Please see my resume for further information.
